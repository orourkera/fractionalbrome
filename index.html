Key questions

How does liquidity work?

Investors can list their shares during quarterly exit windows. Fractional Home operates a first-look marketplace and helps match buyers and sellers.

What about taxes?

Each property issues annual K-1 statements. We provide guidance and recommended CPAs familiar with fractional ownership structures.

Do I need to be accredited?

We welcome both accredited and sophisticated investors. Certain offerings may require accreditation; we verify during onboarding.

Can I stay in the homes I co-own?

Owners receive discretionary stay credits based on property type and availability without impacting rental yield.
