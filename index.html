Key questions

How does liquidity work?

Co-buyers can trigger a buyout at pre-agreed valuations. Fractional Home will either acquire the share or source a vetted replacement within 45 days.

What about taxes?

We provide pro-rated mortgage interest and expense statements plus access to CPAs who specialize in multi-owner deductions.

Who qualifies to join?

Applicants complete a soft credit pull and income verification. We focus on stable renters ready to step into homeownership.

Do we have to live together?

Most members co-live, but we also support investment-focused co-buying with renters or short-term tenants—your agreement sets the rules.
